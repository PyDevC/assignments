Disaster Response Analysis
Key Tasks:
    • Collect datasets related to natural disasters (e.g., floods, earthquakes).
    • Identify regions at high risk and study response effectiveness.
Tools Required:
    • Python Libraries: Pandas, GeoPandas, Seaborn
Sample Dataset Link:
Disaster Management Dataset
Data Analysis Tasks:
    • Which regions are most prone to specific natural disasters?
    • How have response times improved over the years?
    • What is the average recovery time for various disaster types?
    • Are there patterns in casualty rates based on infrastructure quality?
    • Which response methods are most effective in reducing damage?
    • How does socioeconomic status impact recovery rates?
    • What additional preparedness steps can reduce future risks?
